Refund Policy

Last updated 23 June 2026 · v1.0

suko is a one-time purchase, and we want it to be an easy one. You can try the full app for free before you ever pay, and if you do buy and change your mind, you have 30 days to get every cent back, no questions asked.

The short version

  • 30 days, no questions asked. Email us within 30 days of purchase and we'll refund you in full.
  • Try before you buy. The app is free to download and use first, so you can confirm it fits before paying.
  • Full amount, back to your card. Refunds go to your original payment method, processed by Paddle, our payment provider.
  • One email is all it takes. Write to hello@suko.pro with your license key and you're done.

1. The 30-day guarantee

If you buy a suko license and decide within 30 days that it isn't right for you, you're entitled to a full refund. You don't need to explain why, "changed my mind" is a perfectly good reason. We'd love to hear what didn't work so we can improve, but feedback is never a condition of your refund.

The 30-day window starts on the date of your purchase.

2. How to request a refund

Email hello@suko.pro from the address you used to buy, and include your license key or order number. That's it.

  • We confirm your order and approve the refund, usually within one business day.
  • Paddle, our payment provider and merchant of record, returns the funds to your original payment method.
  • The refunded license key is deactivated.

3. When the money arrives

Once a refund is approved, Paddle issues it right away. How quickly it appears on your statement depends on your bank or card provider, typically 5 to 10 business days. PayPal and most wallets are usually faster. If it's been longer than that, get in touch and we'll chase it with Paddle for you.

4. After the 30 days

Because suko is a one-time purchase that includes free updates for life, we don't run a renewal you could be surprised by, there's nothing to cancel and no recurring charge. That's also why the guarantee is a fixed 30-day window rather than an open-ended one.

If something stops working after the 30 days, that's support, not a refund question, and we'll fix it. Email us and we'll make it right.

5. Your statutory rights

This guarantee is offered in addition to any rights you have under the consumer-protection laws of your country. Nothing here limits those rights. Where local law grants you a stronger or longer right to a refund, that law applies.

6. Questions

Not sure whether your situation qualifies? Just ask, we'd rather you reach out than wonder. Email hello@suko.pro and a human will reply.
